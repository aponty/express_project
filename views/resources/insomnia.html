Insomnia sucks. Here's an intro on how to deal with it.

disease facts


Solutions:

cbt; long term fix

finding the patterns of behavior and thought that lead to issues, and changing them. That's what this is for!

SHORT TERM FIXES

drugs. diphenhydramine works, and other OTC drugs as well. There are also prescription medicines as well, but that's a topic for your doctor.

meditation: quell that brewin' thought-storm up in there

exercise

if you've been laying in bed for 20 min or so, and sleep isn't coming, don't just lay there. Get up, meditate, accept that this happens and you'll be ok (that frustration of knowing tomorrow is going to suck is a contributing factor, annoyingly) go for a walk if you really need to.

Then try again.
